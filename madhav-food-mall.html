Kya Chahiye Partner Banne Ke Liye?

FRANCHISE REQUIREMENTS

Simple standards. Big returns. Amara partner banna easy che — bas in basic cheezon ka dhyan rakho.

📍 Location

High footfall area — near school, market, hospital, station, or highway. Minimum 80 sqft for cart setup.

💰 Investment Ready

Initial capital as per selected plan. 30% must be self-funded. Loan assistance available on request.

🎯 Brand Standards

Only Madhav-approved menu, pricing, packaging & uniforms. No modifications without HQ approval.

📋 Legal & Hygiene

FSSAI license mandatory. Clean, hygienic setup as per Madhav standards. Regular audits done.

👤 Operator Presence

Owner or trained manager must be on-site daily. Cannot be run fully by unskilled staff.

📱 Reporting

Weekly sales report via WhatsApp. Monthly targets set. Support team always available 9am–9pm.
